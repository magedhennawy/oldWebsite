About Myself

I'm a computer scientist and designer.

I've always been fascinated with science and technology. It's been the driving force in nearly everything I do and Computer Science is a great way for me to focus this thirst for knowledge. I study at the University of Toronto where I also work as a software developer on our in-house auto marker.

In school, I study everything from project management to building soccer-playing robots. Embedded systems is one of my favourite topics and tinkering with motors, sensors and code is a small hobby of mine. I'm also a part-time software developer for the university, where I mostly do web development.

I do a lot of work with code and tech; sometimes it can be a little overwhelming and I need to step back oftem. I unwind by drawing logos or illustrations in Adobe Illustrator or even going out and taking photos of the city. But sometimes I just need to completely unplug.
